About This Project

What is the Chitechma Student Marketplace?

The Chitechma Student Marketplace is a web platform for students of Chitechma University Institute, Buea to buy and sell items within the school community. Students can register, list products with photos, browse listings and contact sellers directly — all without leaving campus.

The Problem We Are Solving

Chitechma students often struggle to buy or sell used items. Common problems include:

  • No organised place to advertise items on campus
  • Relying on disorganised WhatsApp groups
  • Difficulty finding affordable items from trusted fellow students
  • Items going to waste when they could be reused
